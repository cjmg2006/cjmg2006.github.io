About

Empathy, creativity and authenticity are the heart of my craft. I am fascinated by how technology can transform the way we learn from the world and each other.

I'm a final-year student studying product design and human-computer interaction at Stanford. I have designed things both in-house @ AltSchool and NovoEd, and in design consultancy settings @ fuseproject and d.school K-12 Lab Network.

I'm a full-stack designer, focusing on digital UI/UX and design research. I enjoy making physical things in the machine shop too, and have a deep interest in designing for education. Read this article I wrote to learn why.

Feel free to check out my resume and contact me at cjmg@stanford.edu!
